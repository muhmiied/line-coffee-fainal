# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Line Coffee has two primary user groups: customers discovering and ordering
premium coffee, and the Line Coffee owner/team managing products, content,
media, orders, and analytics. Customers need a warm, trustworthy, bilingual
coffee experience. The owner needs a clear operating system that controls the
brand without code.

## Product Purpose

Line Coffee Final / Visual Lab exists to become the future Line Coffee
ecosystem: public website, premium ecommerce experience, content system, Media
Studio, dashboard, analytics, custom builders, and future mobile app content
source. Success means every surface is premium, bilingual, reusable,
CMS-ready, and prepared for future Supabase binding.

## Brand Personality

Warm, precise, and boutique. The interface should feel premium and calm without
becoming generic luxury, cheap ecommerce, or template SaaS.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic SaaS dashboards, default ecommerce grids, copied Framer template
layouts, old Line Coffee UI copied one-to-one, harsh white admin panels, loud
animations, and one-off content structures that cannot become CMS-managed.

## Design Principles

- Database-first thinking: visible content should be shaped as future data.
- Media-first ownership: visuals should be ready to flow through Media Studio.
- Bilingual by default: Arabic and English are first-class surfaces.
- Premium restraint: warm contrast, clear hierarchy, and no decorative noise.
- Operating-system clarity: dashboard patterns should feel powerful, fast, and safe.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Aim for WCAG AA contrast, visible focus states, keyboard-friendly primitives,
reduced-motion support, readable Arabic RTL layouts, and touch targets that
work comfortably on mobile.
